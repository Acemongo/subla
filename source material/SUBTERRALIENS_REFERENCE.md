## 🎲 CORE MECHANICS

### Roll Formula
```
STAT + Special Focus + d10% = RESULT
```
- **d10%** = roll d10, multiply by 10 → range 10–100
- **Opposed:** both sides roll, highest wins. Ties: re-roll d10.
- **Static:** beat GM's `d10% + Difficulty` (Easy 20 / Medium 40 / Hard 60)
- **Saving throw:** `STAT + d10%` vs `Threat Rank + d10%`

### Primary Stats
| Stat | Domain | Example |
|---|---|---|
| RUMBLE | Hand-to-hand / melee | Street fighting ability |
| AGILITY | Acrobatics, dodging, shooting | Initiative, number of actions |
| MIGHT | Strength | Lifting, grappling, movement |
| MOXIE | Endurance, toughness | Resisting toxins, pushing limits |
| SMARTS | Knowledge, brainpower | Hacking, identifying specimens |
| PERCEPTION | Awareness | Noticing hidden threats |
| SPIRIT | Willpower | Resisting fear, possession |

### Stat Ranks
| Roll | Rank | Value |
|---|---|---|
| 1–6 | Average | 8 |
| 7–8 | Decent | 10 |
| 9–10 | Excellent | 20 |
| — | Extraordinary | 30 (Spiritus peak) |
| — | Pathetic | 2 |

### Secondary Stats
- **HEALTH** = RUMBLE + AGILITY + MIGHT + MOXIE
- **WILD** = SMARTS + PERCEPTION + SPIRIT
  - Spend in multiples of 10 to boost rolls (before) or soak damage (after)
  - Restores each session

### Actions & Movement
- **Initiative:** AGILITY + d10% (highest goes first)
- **Actions/turn:** AGILITY ÷ 10 (min 1)
  - Revolver: 1 action | Semi-auto: 2 | Auto: 3
  - Extra action: spend 10 WILD
- **Movement:** MIGHT ÷ 2 yards/round
- **Jump:** MIGHT feet
- **Teamwork bonus:** +10 per collaborating player

### Damage Types
- **Lethal:** guns, blades — 0 HEALTH = death
- **Stun:** bats, clubs, slams — 0 HEALTH = knocked out
- **Armor:** subtracts from incoming damage

## 🌐 WORLD LORE (Summary)

- **The Progenitor:** Cosmic device that crashed into early Earth. Contact with it created Subterraliens — Hominoids, Mineraliens, Spiritus, Vegetoids. Also transformed early humans into the **High Lords**.
- **The Deep:** Subterranean biomes — bioluminescent caverns, crystal chambers, ancient ruins. Alive with alien ecosystems.
- **The Pangaea Atlas:** Atlantean device that controlled biome shifting; kept threats contained for centuries.
- **Abaddon's Hades Kingdom:** Deep realm where Progenitor growth chambers flourish.
- **City of the Lizard People (Los Angeles):** A biome once located under modern LA City Hall. The pyramid atop City Hall is a marker. The Elysium Vault — a 1940s–50s elite bunker below the city — was repurposed by the Cult of the Deep as a Subterralien research lab. Sealed after the 1994 Northridge earthquake.
- **Subterralien Races:** Not all are evil. Some Vegetoids prefer surface life. All share a common desire to rule the surface.
